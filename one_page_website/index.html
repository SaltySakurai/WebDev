Grace Street: Caffeine & Sweets, What a Delight

Coffee, Lattes, Waffles, & Treats! College Student Study Playground!

Grace Street is probably the best hipster joint to study with your friends while drinking lattes, coffee,and eating absurdly sweet treats such as waffles as well. Located in the heart of Koreatown, it is also a rather popular tourist spot as well! Inspired by Korean coffeehouses, Grace Street gives a more welcoming vibe during the day but a dessert parlor feel in the late hours of the night. Whether you are the early bird or the night owl, Grace Street is great for all types of college students whether they are coffee addicts or have a sweet tooth in general. Don't forget to leave the store without purchasing merch as shameless plugs for them while you use their tote bag on the street and you are paid nothing for advertising for them.
